Tenant Details

The AB1245 Group was established in 1993 as an accounting and business consulting firm catering to independently owned medical practices. In 1996, Medical Billing Services were integrated into the firm, prompted by numerous clients' dissatisfaction with their billing vendors; they requested them to provide these services.

Over the years, the AB1245 Group has broadened its scope beyond medical practices to encompass other professional service companies, including engineers, architects, lawyers, dentists, veterinarians, as well as other small businesses, emerging start-up firms, executives, and small business owners.

To complete their transformation into a full-service small business firm, they introduced the Financial Planning division. This in-house Financial Planning division allows their departments to collaborate effectively, seeking optimal solutions for each client. The AB1245 Group proudly holds membership in both the Nexa Chamber of Commerce and the Senia Chamber of Commerce.
